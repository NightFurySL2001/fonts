Tina Anderson is a software engineer and type designer based in Australia. She has primarily worked as a freelance programmer involved with various business applications and became interested in educational app development for Little Australian Schoolies as her grandchildren came of school age. Tina is the typeface designer of the Australian School Handwriting Fonts which is now included in the Google Fonts collection and made available to parents and teachers Australia wide.
